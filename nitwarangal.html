About NITW

The National Institute of Technology Warangal (NITW) is one of the premier technical institutions in India. Established in 1959, it has been at the forefront of engineering education and research. With a vibrant campus life and cutting-edge facilities, NITW provides students with a holistic environment to excel in academics, extracurricular activities, and research.
